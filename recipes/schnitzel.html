Wiener Schnitzel

Description

Again, I am making up this recipe. In real life I have never made wiener schnitzel before. I will teach you how you can do it, though.

Please take a good look at how this recipe is made.

Ingredients

  • Meat, veal if you can find it, I couldn't
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Beat your meat until flat
  2. Dip your meat in flour
  3. Then dip it in eggs
  4. Then then toss it in the bowl of breadcrumbs until fully covered
  5. Fry it in hot oil or lard
  6. AFter frying salt it immediately then let it rest for a couple minutes
